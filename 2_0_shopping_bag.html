Checkout button is enabled only if at least 1 item is checked.


Shopping rule: User can only choose no more than 10 different products, and each for no more than 3 quantity.


When clicking on CHECKOUT button, the system will validate the stock of the chosen products in the shopping bag. If there is stock info change for the ticked products, a popup shows to remind user that the page will refresh.


If the shopping bag is refreshed, manually or automatically, all the checkboxes are cleaned (unselected state).


The product stock info change may include:

• stock info update (stock decreased to the number less than the user chose. Say, a user chose 3 qty for a product previously. The next time he opened the shopping bag (or when the page is refreshed), the stock for the product changed to 2.) 

• Out of Stock (temporarily, the mall will refill the stock later)

• Sold Out (the mall will not refill the stock and will not sell this product any more). 


Case for stock info update: If the product stock decreased to a number less than the quantity the user chose previously, an error message shows below the quantity selector, and the checkbox for this product is disabled unless the user changed the quantity manually.
